Japanesse Cheesecake

This oriental cheescake is realy smoth and delicius

Ingredients

  • 200g of cream cheese
  • 20g of the butter of your preference
  • 50ml of the milk you prefer
  • 3 egg yolk
  • 30g of wheat flour
  • 3 egg whites
  • 55g of sugar
  • A litle of icing sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot wiht boiling water
  2. In one bowl add the cream cheese, the butter and the milk and start to gently mix all
  3. Put the bowl up to the boiling water pot
    • Check the temperature not be higher than 50 degrees Celsius
  4. Add the yolks and continue the mixing
  5. Add the flour previously strain it, continue to mix it bot more gently from here
  6. Strain the mix
  7. Put the mix in the freezer on till the temperature reach the 10 degrees Celsius
  8. In another bowl add tge egg whites and the sugar, whip the merengue
  9. Save it in the freezer
  10. Prepare the baking pan oiled with butter
  11. Mix 1/3 of the merengue with the cheese mix, then add the rest of the merengue and mix it all
  12. Put all the mix in the baking pan
  13. Put the baking pan in a larger pan with hot water
  14. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes at 140-150 degrees Celsius
  15. After the 40-50 min. raise the temperature at 180 degrees for 10 to 15 more minutes, we want a golden finish in the crust
  16. Turn off the oven and keep the cheescake in there by 10 minutes more
  17. Ready! you can enjoy it warm or cool it too
