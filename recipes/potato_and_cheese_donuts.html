Cheese and Potato Donuts

This crispy and salty donuts will blow your mind

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 big onion
  • 250g of Mozzarella cheese
    • another good option will be manchego cheese
  • 3 spoons of cornstarch
  • 200g of wheat flour
  • 1 little bag of breadcrumb
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and cut in cubes the potatoes
  2. Cook the potatoes in water until they are full coked
  3. Drain the water and add a little spoon of salt and some ground pepper
  4. Mash the potatoes
  5. Add 3 spoons of breadcrumb, mix and reserve
  6. Cut the onion on thick slices
    • We will use the round slices like molds for our donuts
  7. Cut the cheese in strips as wide as the onion slices and as thick as 1/4 inch
  8. Put the cheese inside the onion slices, in the inner side
  9. With the mash potato make small balls
  10. Make one ball and knead it wile making a long strip
  11. Place this strip in the inside of one onion-cheese slice, covering all the inner part, then all the outer part and then all the empty spaces that was left
  12. Turn out the like-donut and cover all the empty spaces of the other side, forming a perfect covered donut!
  13. Repeat this with all the onion-cheese slices
  14. Prepare a little bowl with 4 spoons of flour
  15. In another little bowl crumble the eggs, add 1/3 of salt and mix them
  16. Put some of the breadcrumb in another little bowl
    • Now we have three bowls, the flour, the eggs and the breadcrumb
  17. Take each donut and cover it with flour, take care of any space be well covered
  18. The next step is to bath one floured donut in the mixed egg, and then cover it all with the breadcrumb
  19. Put one pan on medium flame and put in enough kitchen oil
  20. Fry the donuts until they are golden enough, dont burn them!
  21. Once fry it put them in a plate with kitchen paper
  22. And they are ready! you can enjoy them with ketchup or the dressing of your preference
